The VideoText

A speedrunning guide for beating Minecraft as fast as possible.



I selected the VideoText for my portfolio because I spent the most time on this TGE. Many hours went into rerecording footage and polishing the final video, and I'm very proud of what I created! I learned a lot about the entire video production process, from

  • recording footage
  • recording voiceover
  • searching for video editing software
  • learning how to use said software
  • cutting, editing, and compiling the footage together with the voiceover
  • finding images online to put into the video
  • finding copyright-free music
  • exporting a video
  • uploading to YouTube

In terms of class concepts, I took special note of considering my audience. I aimed this video mainly for people who have a medium understanding of Minecraft, as that would allow me to (1) not have to explain every small detail about the game, (2) consequently keep the video within the five minute limit, and (3) maintain a fast-paced video which fits with the theme of speedrunning. In addition, I added a lot of images and on-screen text to aid the viewer in understanding the key points of the video. This also helps maintain the audience's interest and understanding.
